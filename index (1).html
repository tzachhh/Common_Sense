What is the Nick Shirley Act / AB 2624?

AB 2624, authored by Assemblymember Mia Bonta, expands California address-confidentiality protections for immigration support service providers, employees, and volunteers who say they face threats, harassment, or violence because of their work.

Critics call it dangerous because they believe the language could discourage public scrutiny, citizen journalism, independent investigations, and reporting about organizations receiving taxpayer funding. They argue the bill may make it harder to identify and report on individuals and organizations involved in controversial activities.
